Our Key Services in Singapore

We install automatic fire sprinkler systems designed for maximum coverage and compliance. Our sprinkler solutions help suppress fires in their early stages, protecting lives and property. We handle end-to-end services — from piping layout and pump connection to sprinkler head installation, testing, and certification.

Engg-Tech Construction provides certified fire alarm system installation across Singapore. Our solutions include smoke detectors, heat sensors, strobe lights, and manual call points to ensure early detection and reliable alerts in case of fire emergencies. We install and commission systems that comply with SCDF standards for residential, commercial, and industrial properties.

Engg-Tech offers integrated M&E services covering HVAC systems, electrical works, plumbing, fire safety, and more. We specialize in mechanical and electrical system design, installation, and maintenance to support smooth building operations and compliance with regulatory standards.

We provide end-to-end general construction services including structural works, building renovations, roofing, and civil projects. Trusted solutions tailored to meet Singapore's construction standards and client expectations.

From minor upgrades to full interior transformations, our renovation team helps clients improve functionality and design. We offer hacking, tiling, carpentry, partitioning, and painting services for HDBs, condos, offices, and retail outlets — all delivered with quality workmanship and on-time schedules.

Need quick fixes or home repairs? Our skilled handymen are ready to assist with minor works such as door hinge fixes, shelf mounting, lock replacements, light fixture changes, and other general maintenance tasks for residential and commercial clients.

We provide residential and commercial electrical installation services in Singapore, including power point setup, lighting installation, DB box rewiring, and power upgrades. All works are performed by qualified electricians and meet SP Group and EMA standards for safety and efficiency.

Our licensed plumbers provide reliable water supply and sanitary installation works in homes, offices, and commercial spaces. We handle everything from leak repair and pipe replacement to toilet bowl installation, sink fitting, and full plumbing upgrades — all according to PUB regulations.
